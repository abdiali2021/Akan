The Akan people are a meta-ethnicity living primarily in the countries of present-day Ghana and Ivory Coast.

The akan are the largest ethnic group in Ghana

The Akan name generator calculates your birth date and gives you your akan name depending on your gender and the day of the week you were born.

Male names were Kwasi, Kwadwo, Kwabena, Kwaku, Yaw, Kofi and Kwame.

Female names were Akosua, Adwoa, Abenaa, Akua, Yaa, Afua and Ama.
